10 Tips for Sustainable Practices

  • Reduce meat consumption to minimize greenhouse gas emissions.
  • Use public transport, walk, or bike whenever possible.
  • Use energy-efficient appliances and turn off lights when not in use.
  • Conserve water by taking shorter showers and fixing leaks.
  • Reduce, reuse, and recycle to minimize waste.
  • Buy locally sourced and seasonal produce.
  • Use eco-friendly cleaning and personal care products.
  • Support renewable energy sources to reduce carbon footprint.
  • Get involved in your community to promote sustainability.
  • Stay informed and educate others about sustainable practices.
