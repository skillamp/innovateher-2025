10 Tips for Sustainable Practices

  1. Reduce meat consumption to minimize greenhouse gas emissions
  2. Use public transport, walk, or bike whenever possible
  3. Use energy-efficient appliances and turn off lights when not in use
  4. Conserve water by taking shorter showers and fixing leaks
  5. Reduce, reuse, and recycle to minimize waste
  6. Buy locally sourced and seasonal produce
  7. Use eco-friendly cleaning products and personal care products
  8. Support renewable energy sources and reduce carbon footprint
  9. Get involved in your community to promote sustainability
  10. Stay informed and educate others about sustainable practices
